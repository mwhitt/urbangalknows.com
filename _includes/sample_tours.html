Here are a few successful tours we have delivered in the past:

Bike Tour

San Francisco is famous for its hills but you won't go there. Walking is allowed so don't think you need to be a world class cyclist. The ride starts at Fishreman's Wharf then you'll ride through the Marina, Crissy Fields, the Golden Gate Bridge and on to Sausalito. Riding into Sausalito is a blast because of a 2 mile drop into the picturesque little town but don't worry, you're returning to San Francisco on a ferry.

Movie Tour

Have you heard of Dirty Harry or Bullit? The movies were filmed in the streets of San Francisco and if you're a film buff this is the San Francisco tour for you. You'll be taken to the exact locations of the more popular scenes.

Wine Tour

V. Sattui Winery in St. Helena or Domain Chandon in Yountville are the destinations. The wine tasting fees at V. Sattui are $5.00 for a flight of 6 classic wines and $10.00 for a flight of 6 limited release wines. Domain Chandon is a sparkingling wine so the fees are a bit more. The two wineries offer the best packages in terms of wine tasting, a terrific lunch and breathtaking landscape but there are no shortage of wineries along the way. You can stop to wine taste to your heart's delight -- afterall, I'm your designated driver.

Carmel by the sea

Carmel is a world renowned little town frequented by celebrities and jet setters because of it's European style, unique hotels, quaint inns and the Pebble Beach Golf Classic. Oh let's not forget Hollywood isn't far away. Clint Eastwood (Dirty Harry) was once mayor of Carmel and Doris Day (actress/singer from the 50's) is still an owner of the Cypress Inn. The restaurants are world class with international cuisine and there are lots of boutiques for those who love to shop.

The Happy Hour Tour

Need I say more. The tour is a great way for an exhausted traveler to just chill. The coolest neighborhoods have bars with long happy hours or just great prices for a beer or well drink but the best deal is having a designated San Francisco driver.
